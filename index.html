Who we are

Thompson Street advises companies across frontier technology, regulatory policy, and the global financial system.

We believe policy begins with a pull-request. We believe billion dollar markets can launch overnight. We believe builders and founders are not downstream of policy. They are the authors of it.

We enter conversations to change them. We produce original analysis, genuine research, and custom data. We help build products that draw influential partners, policymakers, and journalists to you.

We prefer retainer engagements with a small number of clients. Project-based work if the fit is right. We scope engagements tightly. We bill only for work that matters. We will tell you when you do not have a problem we can solve.
